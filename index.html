About Me

I was born and raised in Alexandria, VA. I attended multiple schools throughout Fairfax County and graduated from J.E.B. Stuart HS. I was accepted to Virginia Tech and studied General Biology. After Undergrad, I worked as a laboratory technician at multiple laboratories throughout Northern Virginia. Currently I work as a histotechnician at a large anatomical pathology laboratory in Bowie, MD.
In my free time I enjoy playing soccer, golf, and home improvement projects. My family lives very close to the Alexandria area and my brother, father, and I play a round of golf or kick the soccer ball around most weekends.
